Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

ScanPro is built with privacy as its core principle. Your documents never leave your device. We do not collect, store, or transmit your files or personal data.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

ScanPro does not collect any of the following:

  • Your documents, photos, or PDF files
  • Personal identification information
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers
  • Usage analytics or behavioural data
  • Camera or microphone data

2. How the App Works

All document scanning and PDF generation happens entirely on your device using your browser's local processing capabilities. No data is uploaded to any server at any point. The app functions completely offline once loaded.

3. Local Storage

The app uses your device's local storage only to remember your daily usage count and Pro status (if applicable). This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us.

4. Third-Party Services

ScanPro uses the following third-party libraries, loaded from CDN:

  • jsPDF (PDF generation — runs locally in your browser)
  • Google Fonts (typography — font files only, no tracking)

These services do not receive your documents or personal information.

5. Children's Privacy

ScanPro is not directed at children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
