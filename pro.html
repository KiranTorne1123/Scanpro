Common questions

How does the license key work? ▼
After payment on Gumroad, you'll receive a license key by email. Paste it into the "Activate Pro" box in the app. Your Pro access is stored locally — no account needed.
Is my data safe? ▼
Yes. ScanPro runs entirely in your browser. Your images and PDFs are never uploaded to any server. Everything happens on your device.
Can I cancel any time? ▼
Absolutely. For the monthly plan, cancel from your Gumroad account and you won't be charged again. The yearly plan is a one-time payment with no auto-renewal unless you opt in.
What payment methods are accepted? ▼
Gumroad accepts all major credit/debit cards, PayPal, and Apple Pay. No account required to check out.
What's the refund policy? ▼
Full refund within 30 days, no questions asked. Just email support and we'll sort it immediately.
